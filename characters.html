Characters

Mabel Pines

Mabel is a 12-year-old cheerful girl, full of energy and enthusiasm. Together with his twin brother Dipper, he spends the summer with his uncle Stan in the town of Gravity falls. During the holidays, the children have to deal with otherworldly manifestations that infest the city.

Dipper Pines

Dipper is 12 years old and just starting to learn about the world around him. Curiosity and curiosity do not allow the teenager to sit still, he constantly gets involved in adventures. Together with his sister, he spends the summer in the town of Gravity falls, where they are haunted by mysterious mystical events.

Stanley Pines

Stan is Dipper and Mabel's uncle, who lives in Gravity Falls. For a long time he was a fraudster and engaged in criminal activities, but later settled in a provincial town of Oregon, where he opened a tourist store "Cabin of Wonders". Initially acts as a skeptic, but it turns out that he is closely connected with the paranormal events.
