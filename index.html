About The Animated TV Series

Who among us, as a child, did not dream of seeing something mysterious and mysterious? For the twins Dipper and Mabel, this dream became a reality – they became part of the mystical events of Gravity falls. Every day of their vacation in uncle Stan's "Mystery Shack" is full of paranormal riddles that are difficult to solve without logic and common sense, but for brave guys this is not a problem.

Storyline

The adventure of fraternal twins, Dipper and Mabel Pines.

Gravity falls is a small town in Oregon with eccentric residents. Dipper and Mabel are Stan's nephews who have come to stay with him for the summer. The restless twins work in the "Mystery Shack" and almost every day they encounter supernatural creatures of the city.

Alex Hirsch and the creation of his life

Gravity Falls is another animated masterpiece created by Alex Hirsch and Disney.

The original animated series includes 2 seasons, and the first episode was shown in June 2012. Initially, Hirsch's plans included creating 3 seasons, but later he decided to release two, but at the same time leave the opportunity to create a full-length version.

Alex drew Dipper and Mabel from himself and his twin sister Ariel.

As teenagers, they often got involved in adventures, inventing attacks by zombies and ghosts. Some episodes reflect Hirschev's childhood memories, for example, Alex's sister always wanted to have a pig, so in the animated series, Mabel has a puffy One.
